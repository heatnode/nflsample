General Info

Hopefully the sample application is fairly self-explanatory. Here are a few notes:

  • You should see 2 or 3 drop-downs at the top depending on whether you've selected a chart based on one year or all years.

  • The application is based on the idea of starting at a high level (years) and being able to uncover more detail by drilling down.

  • Note that you can click on the yearly level bar charts to get to the weekly level.

  • Clicking on "QB Performance" or "Home" will essentially reset the application.

  • The application is somewhat responsive friendly. It should render in a decent way even on a small device. You can resize your browser window to a very small size to see the effect.

  • I tested in current versions of Chrome and Internet Explorer. Should be fine in any modern-ish browser. IE8 not supported. :)
